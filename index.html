Hello, and thanks for visiting my site.

I've been singing and playing guitar for over 30 years. I enjoy singing songs everyone knows and loves, from the 50's to the 90's. I enjoy learning new songs as well as writing some of my own. I am laid back, easy going and I like that to come through in my performances. I look forward to performing at your next party or event.

A Little About Me . . .

My father who was a singer and guitar player most of his life, gave me my first guitar at 12. While it was hard keeping up with him early on, perseverance kept me coming back to do better. Since then, my love of music has only grown, and I've held on to the gift of music my father nurtured in me. I enjoy playing for a crowd, having them sing along to a songs they love and bringing back memories for them.
